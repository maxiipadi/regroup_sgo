A 3-year mission with 8 crew members at Jezero Crater presents a monumental challenge for resource management.

Our philosophy is simple: "On Mars, there is no 'waste', only raw materials in the wrong place."

Modular System Architecture

Primary Processing Module (PPM)

Classification and initial pre-treatment of all waste materials.

Transformation & Manufacturing Module (TMM)

Conversion of recycled materials into useful products, tools, and components.

Storage & Reuse Module (SRM)

Inventory management and reintegration of new resources into the base.
